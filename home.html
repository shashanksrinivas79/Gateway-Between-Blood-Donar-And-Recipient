Donate Blood Save Life

The main idea of our web page is to help the blood needy people to get blood in less time and help the patient to survive. This idea was developed by M.shashank and N.Sai Shashank

In the present world due to the pandemic lots of people got requried of blood platlets to recover or survive. Due to no proper guidance or way to reach the other people lot's of people had lost their lifes. So our aim is to create a gateway between "Donar and Acceptor" so that there will be less possibility of death.
